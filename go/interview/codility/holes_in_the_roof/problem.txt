Problem:

You are given n binary values x0, x1, . . . , xn−1, such that xi∈{0, 1}.
This array represents holes in a roof (1 is a hole).
You are also given k boards of the same size.
The goal is to choose the optimal (minimal) size of the boards that allows all
the holes to be coveredby boards.
